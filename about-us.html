ABOUT

Cleodon Strategy

The Cleodon Strategy Group is a privately held business consulting company whose principals have helped business owners achieve their goals for over 30 years.

We’ve provided our consultation services to over 150 clients, offering advice for both ongoing and specific projects. We work with startups and mature companies from one employee to 100+. Continue scrolling to learn more about what distinguishes Cleodon Strategy Group.

Customed Tailored Solutions


Our team maintains a flexible yet realistic approach to our clients’ needs. We have no overarching formula through which we jettison each and every client. On the contrary, we understand that every project we take on is unique and requires its own process. We construct your business’ individual solution by thoroughly understanding your issues, your goals, and your overall vision.
