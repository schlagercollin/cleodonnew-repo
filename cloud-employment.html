What is

Cloud Employment

Professionally, self-employment is about independence and freedom, but personally it’s often about compromise. As someone earning 1099 income, you’re well aware of the benefits you’re being denied. Affordable group healthcare, tax deudtible expenses and more. It doesn’t have to be that way!

By leveraging the collective strength of thousands of entreprenuers just like you, the Cloud Employment Program unleashes Fortune 500 benefits for the self-employed.

BE IN BUSINESS FOR YOURSELF, NO LONGER BY YOURSELF.


It’s really quite simple! By enrolling in the program, you become a virtual employee, making yourself eligable for the wide range of perks afforded to thse with W2 income. Your income remains the same. You simply change the way it’s classified. And, throughout, you remain in control.

You decide how much of your gross earnings are reclassified through the program. You make the necessary health, dental and vision designations. You control decisions over 401(k) and Section 125 flexible spending accounts.
